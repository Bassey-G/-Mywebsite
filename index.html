Vanilla cake recipe

Ingredients

1. cooking spray
2. 2 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed
3. 1 cup white sugar
4. 1 tablespoon baking powder
5. 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
6. 2 pinches salt
7. 3 eggs
8. ¾ cup milk
9. ¾ cup vegetable oil
